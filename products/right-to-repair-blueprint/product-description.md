# Product 3: Right to Repair Compliance Kits for Refurbishers

## Product Description

### What You Get
A complete go-to-market blueprint for launching a subscription-based compliance kit service for independent electronics refurbishers — helping them legally repair and resell devices under new Right to Repair laws.

### The Opportunity
Right to Repair laws passed in 4+ US states and the EU in 2023/2024. Independent refurbishers now need standardized repair documentation, safety protocols, and legal compliance materials to stay on platforms like Back Market and Amazon Renewed. No one is providing this as a service.

### What's Inside
- **90-Day Launch Plan** — From asset compilation to marketplace outreach
- **Sales Playbook** — RMA-rate reduction angle + partnership strategy
- **MVP Specification** — Complete "Compliance Kit" structure for each device model
- **Pricing Models** — $49/month subscription + $299 certification audit + $1,000+ custom
- **Risk Mitigation** — No-warranty disclaimers, manufacturer update resiliency

### Target Buyer
Electronics technicians, refurbishers, repair shop owners, or anyone with technical writing/electronics experience wanting to serve the indie refurbisher market.

### Suggested Selling Price

# Product 2: CSRD Data Concierge for Tier 2 Suppliers

## Product Description

### What You Get
A complete go-to-market blueprint for launching a data concierge service for SME manufacturers being hit with ESG data requests from their large corporate clients (Siemens, Volkswagen, IKEA, etc.).

### The Opportunity
EU's CSRD requires large companies to report on their entire supply chain (Scope 3 emissions). These companies are now demanding detailed ESG data from their SME suppliers — who have no tools or expertise to provide it. This is a "managed service" opportunity with $2,500+/year per client potential.

### What's Inside
- **90-Day Launch Plan** — From data asset creation to first paying clients
- **Sales Playbook** — The "Contract Security" angle that closes
- **MVP Specification** — ESG Data Room + Response Engine template
- **Pricing Models** — From $499 single responses to $5,000/year premium
- **Risk Mitigation** — Liability disclaimers and data accuracy safeguards

### Target Buyer
B2B consultants, supply chain professionals, or ESG analysts with manufacturing sector experience looking to serve the underserved "mid-market" of SME suppliers.

# GTM Blueprint: CSRD Data Concierge for Tier 2 Suppliers

## 1. Step-by-Step 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: Data Asset & Workflow Development
*   **Response Bank:** Create standardized response templates for the top 5 ESG frameworks (EcoVadis, CDP, GRI, etc.) tailored for SME manufacturers.
*   **Data Room Template:** Build a secure "ESG Data Room" (Notion or customized SharePoint) for clients to store all certifications (ISO 14001, 9001) and energy/waste data.
*   **Target List:** Build a database of 200 "Tier 2" manufacturers in the automotive and electronics sectors in Germany and the USA (Midwest).

### Days 31-60: Content & Lead Generation
*   **"Survival Guide" Content:** Publish: "The Supplier’s Guide to Surviving Enterprise ESG Audits."
*   **Partnership Outreach:** Contact supply chain software firms (e.g., SAP Ariba, Ivalua) to offer a "concierge service" add-on for their SME users.
*   **Lead Magnet:** "The 15-Minute CSRD Readiness Audit" - a checklist that scores a supplier's risk of contract loss based on their current data.

### Days 61-90: Active Sales & Pilot Delivery
*   **Direct Outreach:** High-volume personalized email and LinkedIn outreach to "Quality Managers" and "Managing Directors."
*   **Pilot Program:** Sign first 3 "Concierge" clients at a discounted rate ($1,500/year) to build case studies focused on "Zero questionnaire stress."
*   **Onboarding:** Standardize the "Data Harvesting" call to take less than 60 minutes of the client's time.

---

## 2. Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Channels & Tactics
*   **LinkedIn Account-Based Marketing (ABM):** Focus on the specific pain of "Questionnaire Fatigue." Target companies with 50-250 employees.
*   **Trade Associations:** Partner with the German BME or the US National Association of Manufacturers (NAM). Offer a free webinar on "Winning Tenders with ESG Data."
*   **SEO:** Target keywords like "EcoVadis questionnaire help," "respond to Siemens ESG audit," and "Scope 3 emissions for small manufacturers."

### Sales Pitch (The "Contract Security" Angle)
> "Managing Director, your big clients (Siemens, Volkswagen) are now legally required to report on their supply chain. They aren't just checking boxes; they are ranking vendors by 'ESG Risk.' If you aren't providing high-quality data, you are a risk to their compliance. We handle all their questionnaires for you, ensuring you keep your contracts and win new ones."

---

## 3. MVP Scope: The "ESG Data Room"
The core value is taking the data burden off the founder. The MVP includes:
*   **The Vault:** A centralized document store for energy bills, waste logs, and labor policies.
*   **Response Engine:** A library of pre-written answers for common ESG platforms.
*   **Gap Analysis:** A report showing which data is missing *before* a client asks for it.

---

## 4. Pricing & Monetization Recommendations
*   **Single Questionnaire Response ($499):** One-off help for a single urgent request.
*   **The Annual Concierge ($2,500/year):** Management of all inbound ESG requests + maintenance of the Data Room + quarterly data updates.
*   **Premium "Audit-Ready" Package ($5,000/year):** Includes a third-party audit of your data to provide a "Certification of Integrity" to your buyers.

---

## 5. Key Risks & Mitigations
*   **Risk: Software Automation.** Big companies might release tools that automate this.
    *   *Mitigation:* Software can't find missing energy bills or draft custom labor policies. Position as "Managed Service," not just a tool.
*   **Risk: Liability for False Data.** 
    *   *Mitigation:* Include strict clauses that the client is responsible for the accuracy of raw data; you are responsible for the *formatting and reporting*.

---

## 6. Resource Requirements
*   **Knowledge:** Deep understanding of ESG frameworks (CSRD, GHG Protocol).
*   **Sales:** B2B experience in industrial/manufacturing sectors.
*   **Tools:** CRM (HubSpot Free), Data Storage (Notion/SharePoint), Cold Email (Apollo.io).

---

## 7. Scaling & KPIs
*   **Target KPIs:** 20% renewal rate on single responses; <4 hours of manual labor per client per quarter.
*   **Scale Strategy:** Hire sector-specific "Data Analysts" (e.g., Food & Bev specialist, Automotive specialist) to increase technical accuracy and authority.

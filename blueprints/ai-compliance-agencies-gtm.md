# GTM Blueprint: AI Compliance Audit for Small Creative Agencies

## 1. Step-by-Step 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: Product & Authority Foundation
*   **Asset Creation:** Develop the "Agency AI Disclosure Standard" (a 5-page template agencies can use with clients).
*   **Lead Magnet:** Build a simple "AI Liability Calculator" (Typeform or simple JS tool) that estimates potential fines under the EU AI Act based on revenue and usage type.
*   **Audit Checklist:** Finalize the 25-point audit checklist covering data sourcing, copyright, and model transparency.
*   **Website:** Simple one-pager focusing on "Enterprise-Grade Compliance for Creative Agencies."

### Days 31-60: Beta Testing & Proof of Concept
*   **Beta Group:** Recruit 5 mid-sized creative agencies (20-50 employees) for a free "Compliance Teardown" in exchange for video testimonials.
*   **Refinement:** Update the audit tool based on common "hidden" AI uses discovered during beta (e.g., shadow AI use by freelancers).
*   **Content:** Write 3 case studies: "How Agency X protected a $200k contract using our compliance audit."

### Days 61-90: Market Launch
*   **LinkedIn Blitz:** Launch targeted ads at "Head of Operations" and "Creative Director" roles.
*   **Webinar:** Host a "Safe AI Creative Workflows" webinar with a guest legal expert.
*   **Sales:** Launch the $149 "Self-Service Audit" and $749 "Managed Compliance Package."

## 2. Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Channels & Tactics
*   **LinkedIn (Account Based Marketing):** Identify 50 high-value agencies. Connect with Ops leads and share the "AI Liability Calculator."
*   **Trade Associations:** Outreach to AIGA and 4As. Offer a "Member Discount" for their community.
*   **Cold Outreach:** Personalized Loom videos showing the agency's current lack of a public AI policy and how it might look to a risk-averse enterprise client.

### Outreach Template (Cold LinkedIn/Email)
> "Subject: Your agency's AI transparency report for [Enterprise Client]
>
> Hi [Name], I noticed [Agency] is doing incredible work with [Project]. With enterprise clients now demanding strict AI disclosure (following the EU AI Act), are you prepared for their next compliance audit? 
>
> We help agencies like yours generate 'Transparency & Compliance Reports' that prove your AI usage is safe and legal. I ran a quick check on your public footprint and have 3 suggestions to reduce your liability. Do you have 10 mins Tuesday?"

## 3. MVP Scope & Detailed Questionnaire
The MVP should focus on the **Compliance Checker tool**. Key audit questions include:
1.  Which AI tools are used for client deliverables? (Midjourney, ChatGPT, etc.)
2.  Do you have "Opt-Out" of training enabled for all seats?
3.  Are freelancers required to disclose AI use in their contracts?
4.  Do you have a human-in-the-loop review process for AI-generated code/assets?
5.  What is your process for verifying that AI-generated content doesn't violate third-party IP?

---

## 4. Pricing & Monetization Recommendations
*   **The "Starter" Audit ($149):** Self-service form → Automated PDF report.
*   **The "Pro" Subscription ($79/month):** Quarterly re-audits + "Certified Safe AI" badge + monthly regulation news.
*   **The "Enterprise" Concierge ($1,500+):** Full manual workflow review + custom Internal AI Policy drafting + Freelancer contract review.

---

## 5. Key Risks & Mitigations
*   **Risk: Legal Liability.** You are providing "compliance advice," not legal advice.
    *   *Mitigation:* Use strict disclaimers. Position as "Process Audit" rather than "Legal Certification."
*   **Risk: Platform Risk.** AI tools might bake in their own compliance reports.
    *   *Mitigation:* Focus on the *agency's workflow* (multi-tool usage) which individual platform reports can't cover.

---

## 6. Resource Requirements
*   **Technical:** No-code stack (Carrd for landing page, Typeform for audit, Make.com for PDF generation).
*   **Legal:** A one-time consultation with an IP/Tech lawyer to validate the 25-point checklist ($1,000 budget).
*   **Marketing:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator subscription ($100/mo).

---

## 7. Scale Strategy & KPIs
*   **Target KPIs:** 15% conversion from Free Calculator to Paid Audit; $2,500 LTV for Concierge clients.
*   **Scaling:** Once 20 agencies are certified, create a "Compliance Directory" where enterprise clients can find safe vendors.

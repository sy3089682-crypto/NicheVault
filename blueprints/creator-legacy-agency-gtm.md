# GTM Blueprint: Creator Legacy & Sunset Agency

## 1. Step-by-Step 90-Day Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Product Definition & Legal Framework (Days 1-30)
- **Legal Architecture:** Work with digital estate lawyers to draft the "Brand Living Trust" and "IP Transition" templates for platforms like YouTube, Substack, and Kajabi.
- **AI Protocol Beta:** Develop a simple "Voice & Logic" library for a creator (using tools like ElevenLabs for voice and HeyGen for video cloning) to test archival up-cycling.
- **Service Tiering:** Define exactly what goes into the $4,500 "Legacy Audit."

### Phase 2: High-Value Partnerships & Alpha (Days 31-60)
- **Alpha Testing:** Partner with 2 "Burned Out" mid-tier creators (500k-1M subs) to build their legacy plan for free in exchange for a case study.
- **Accountant/Agent Outreach:** Pitch the service to 10 top influencer accountants as a "value-add" for their clients.
- **Content Strategy:** Launch "The Creator Exit" podcast and blog.

### Phase 3: Market Launch (Days 61-90)
- **VidCon/Cannes Lions:** Present the "Legacy Framework" at a private workshop for creators.
- **Public Launch:** Open the "Sunset Vault" software for early access.
- **Direct Sales:** Targeted outbound to creators who have publicly mentioned burnout or wanting to "move on."

---

## 2. Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Channels & Tactics
- **Professional Referral Network:** Partner with accountants, talent managers, and M&A platforms for creators.
- **Thought Leadership Content:** "The Creator Exit" podcast/blog targeting the "Business of Influence."
- **Direct Outreach:** Personalized audit teasers for creators who have hit the 5-7 year mark (the "burnout window").
- **Industry Workshops:** High-ticket, private planning sessions for agencies and talent houses.

### Estimated Costs
- **Legal Templating:** $5,000 (specialized legal drafting)
- **AI Development/Integration:** $3,000
- **Marketing/Podcast Production:** $1,000
- **Travel/Events:** $2,000

---

## 3. MVP Scope
- **The Legacy Audit:** A comprehensive PDF report covering IP status, contract gaps, and retirement valuation.
- **The "Sunset Vault":** A secure platform to store master files, brand guidelines, and AI-voice models for future use.
- **IP Transition Templates:** Ready-to-sign legal documents for account/contract transfer.
- **Logic Capture:** A guided interview process to capture the creator's "content philosophy" for future AI content generation.

---

## 4. Pricing & Monetization Recommendations
- **Legacy Audit (High-Touch):** $4,500 one-time fee.
- **The Sunset Vault (SaaS):** $49/month (Recurring revenue for maintenance and updates).
- **Active Legacy Management:** 5% of all post-retirement revenue generated by the brand.
- **Consultancy Retainer:** $500/hour for high-tier IP negotiation.

---

## 5. Key Risks & Mitigations
- **Platform Terms of Service:** YouTube or AdSense might change rules regarding "AI-assisted" legacy content.
    - *Mitigation:* Focus the legal strategy on IP ownership and platform-independent assets (Newsletters, Websites).
- **Reputational Risk:** Fans might react poorly to "Ghost" AI content.
    - *Mitigation:* Clear "Archive-Plus" labeling and ensuring the creator's "Living Will" includes specific instructions on what is/isn't allowed.

---

## 6. Resource Requirements
- **Digital Estate Lawyer:** Specialized in creator contracts and IP.
- **AI Implementation Specialist:** To manage the voice/logic capture protocols.
- **Business Strategist/Accountant:** To handle channel valuation and exit planning.
- **Content Producer:** To manage the agency's own marketing and podcast.

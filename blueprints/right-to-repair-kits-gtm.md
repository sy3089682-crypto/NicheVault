# GTM Blueprint: Right to Repair Compliance Kits for Refurbishers

## 1. Step-by-Step 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: Asset Compilation & Core Content
*   **The "Core Four" Kits:** Develop professional repair, safety, and documentation kits for the 4 highest-volume refurbished models: iPhone 13, iPhone 14, MacBook Air M1, and Samsung Galaxy S22.
*   **Safety Framework:** Create a "Li-ion Battery Safety & Handling Standard" for commercial refurbishing centers.
*   **Legal Compliance Map:** A state-by-state and EU-wide summary of what a refurbisher *must* disclose to be legally compliant under new laws.

### Days 31-60: Community Validation & Trust Building
*   **Beta Launch:** Provide free access to the "Core Four" kits to 10 independent repair shops in exchange for feedback on shop-floor usability and safety protocols.
*   **Partnership Outreach:** Approach a major parts wholesaler (e.g., Mobile Sentrix or Injured Gadgets) to discuss a "Documentation + Parts" co-marketing deal.
*   **Visual Assets:** Design the "Certified Indie Refurbisher" (CIR) digital seal and a "Repair Certificate" template for customers.

### Days 61-90: Market Entry & Monetization
*   **Marketplace Outreach:** Direct outreach to "Silver" and "Gold" tier sellers on Back Market and Swappa who need to lower their RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) rates.
*   **SEO & Social:** Launch a "Repair Compliance" blog and YouTube series. Target keywords like "legal requirements for reselling refurbished iPhones."
*   **Sales:** Launch the $49/month "Compliance Library" and the $299 "Audit Seal."

## 3. MVP Scope: The "Compliance Kit"
Each kit must contain:
1.  **Step-by-Step SOP:** Technical repair instructions with high-res photos.
2.  **Safety Checklist:** Battery health and charging cycle validation protocols.
3.  **Customer Handover Doc:** A template to give the end-buyer, proving the repair was done to standard.
4.  **Legal Disclosure Clause:** Specific text to protect the refurbisher from liability if the user later damages the device.

## 4. Pricing & Monetization Recommendations
*   **"The Library" Subscription ($49/month):** Unlimited access to all model-specific kits + monthly legal updates.
*   **"Certified Indie Refurbisher" (CIR) Audit ($299 one-time):** A manual review of a shop's facility and protocols to grant the CIR seal of approval.
*   **Custom Enterprise Kit ($1,000+):** Custom documentation for niche hardware (e.g., E-bikes, Industrial tools).

---

## 5. Key Risks & Mitigations
*   **Risk: Manufacturer Updates.** Apple/Samsung might change designs to break your SOPs.
    *   *Mitigation:* Focus on the *safety and liability* documentation, which is more stable than specific hardware screws.
*   **Risk: Repair Quality Liability.** 
    *   *Mitigation:* Strict "No-Warranty" disclaimers. You are providing *documentation*, not guaranteeing the repair outcome.

# Niche Report: Creator Legacy & Sunset Agency

## Description
A hybrid consultancy and software platform designed for "The Longevity of Influence." It helps mid-to-high tier digital creators (YouTubers, Newsletter writers, Podcasters) design a "Digital Estate and Brand Legacy Plan." This goes beyond simple password management to include:
1. **The Brand Living Trust:** Legal frameworks for the transition of intellectual property, AdSense accounts, and affiliate contracts.
2. **AI Sunset Protocols:** Creating a library of the creator's voice, face, and "logic" to allow the channel to continue producing "Archive-Plus" content (re-edited/up-cycled archival footage with new AI commentary) after the creator retires or passes.
3. **Monetization Continuity:** Setting up automated systems to manage ongoing royalties and brand deal fulfillment.

## Why it has zero/low competition
- **Legal/Tech Silos:** Traditional estate lawyers don't understand how a YouTube algorithm or a Substack payout works. "Digital Legacy" apps from Apple/Google are focused on data recovery, not *brand management*.
- **The "New Wealth" Problem:** The first generation of career creators is only now reaching the point of needing "Retirement" or "Legacy" planning.
- **Creator Taboo:** Talking about the end of a brand is currently a psychological barrier that most managers (CAA/WME) aren't equipped to handle sensitively.

## Demand signals
- **Industry Giants' Discourse:** High-profile creators (MrBeast, MKBHD) have publicly discussed their channels lasting "100 years" or using AI to translate their content into every language.
- **Creator Burnout:** A rising trend of creators "quitting" or "sunsetting" their channels (e.g., MatPat, Tom Scott) without a clear strategy for the brand's long-term passive value.
- **Search Trends:** Rising search volume for "selling a youtube channel" and "intellectual property for influencers."

## Estimated willingness to pay
- **Legacy Audit:** $4,500 (one-time comprehensive plan).
- **The Sunset Vault (Software):** $49/month to keep the brand's "AI Soul" and legal documents updated as the channel evolves.
- **Agency Management:** 5% of ongoing "Legacy Revenue" for managing the post-retirement operations.

## First-mover advantages
- **Trust Authority:** Becoming the "Family Office" of the creator economy.
- **Proprietary AI Training:** Building a unique dataset on how creator brands actually function and how to automate their legacy.

## Recommended go-to-market approach
- **Targeted Professional Outreach:** Partner with "Influencer Accountants" and agencies like Night Media or Jellysmack.
- **"The Creator Exit" Podcast:** A content series interviewing creators who have successfully sold or retired their brands.
- **Workshops:** Host private planning sessions at major industry events like VidCon or Cannes Lions.

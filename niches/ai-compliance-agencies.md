# Niche Report: AI Compliance Audit for Small Creative Agencies

## Description
A specialized audit service and tool that evaluates a small creative agency's internal AI usage (e.g., Midjourney for assets, ChatGPT for copy, Copilot for code) and generates a structured "Transparency & Compliance Report". This report can be shared with enterprise clients to prove adherence to emerging regulations like the EU AI Act and to resolve copyright/liability concerns.

## Why it has zero/low competition
- **Big Law/Consulting Gap:** Enterprise compliance firms (Deloitte, etc.) focus on Fortune 500 companies.
- **Developer Focus:** Existing AI safety tools (like Arthur or Fiddler) are built for *ML engineers* building models, not for *agencies using* third-party AI tools.
- **Fragmented Standards:** There is currently no "standard" for how an agency should disclose AI usage to a client, creating a "wild west" where clients are becoming increasingly paranoid.

## Demand signals
- **Regulatory Pressure:** The EU AI Act mandates transparency for AI-generated content (Article 52). Agencies serving EU clients are legally exposed.
- **Enterprise Paranoria:** Major brands (e.g., Apple, Samsung) have restricted internal AI use and are now extending those restrictions to their vendors/agencies.
- **Signal:** Search volume for "AI usage disclosure template" and "agency AI liability insurance" is rising in industry forums like AdAge and Digiday.

## Estimated willingness to pay
- **Tier 1 (Audit Tool):** $99 per audit/report.
- **Tier 2 (Subscription):** $49/month for ongoing monitoring of new AI tools added to the workflow.

## First-mover advantages
- **The "Trust Mark":** Becoming the recognized third-party validator for "Ethical & Compliant AI" in the creative industry.
- **Network Effects:** As more agencies use the report, clients start *demanding* it from all their vendors.

## Recommended go-to-market approach
- **Direct Outreach:** Target "Head of Operations" or "General Counsel" at mid-sized creative agencies (20–100 employees).
- **Partnerships:** Partner with professional associations like the 4As or AIGA.
- **Lead Magnet:** Offer a free "AI Liability Calculator" that shows an agency's potential fine exposure under the EU AI Act.

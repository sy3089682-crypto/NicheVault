# Niche Report: Heritage Sketch-to-Build AI

## Description
A specialized AI-powered CAD tool for hobbyist woodworkers and furniture restorers. Users upload a single photograph of an antique piece (from a museum, auction site, or estate sale) or a hand-drawn sketch. The AI uses computer vision trained on historical furniture joinery to generate:
1. A complete 3D model with interactive exploded views.
2. Step-by-step cutting plans and a bill of materials (BOM).
3. Specific joinery guidance (e.g., suggesting proper mortise and tenon dimensions based on the wood species selected).

## Why it has zero/low competition
- **Generalist vs. Vertical AI:** Existing AI design tools focus on "generative" art or generic floor plans. They don't understand the "physics" of wood joinery or the specific aesthetics of heritage furniture (Queen Anne, Shaker, Mid-Century Modern).
- **Pro Software Complexity:** Tools like AutoCAD or Rhino are too complex for the average hobbyist. SketchUp is manual and time-consuming.
- **The "Unstructured Data" Gap:** There is a vast archive of furniture history in non-digital formats (old books, museum photos) that this tool digitizes for the modern maker.

## Demand signals
- **"Build This" Requests:** High frequency of posts in r/woodworking and r/furnituremaking asking for plans based on a single photo of an expensive antique.
- **Micro-Influencer Trend:** Massive growth of "Heritage Making" and "Restoration" channels on YouTube (e.g., Ishitani Furniture, My Self Reliance) creating a surge in aspirational builders.
- **Market Growth:** The global DIY woodworking market is projected to grow significantly as Gen Z and Millennials seek "offline" tactile hobbies.

## Estimated willingness to pay
- **Per Plan:** $14.99 for a one-off generated plan.
- **Pro Hobbyist Subscription:** $19/month for unlimited conversions and access to a "Heritage Library" of vetted designs.

## First-mover advantages
- **Proprietary Joinery Model:** Building the world's first AI model that understands the relationship between furniture style, wood species, and structural integrity.
- **Community Lock-in:** By hosting the generated plans in a public/private "Digital Woodshop," the platform becomes the default repository for high-quality maker data.

## Recommended go-to-market approach
- **Influencer Partnerships:** Provide the tool to 10-15 mid-sized woodworking YouTubers to use in their "How I built this" videos.
- **SEO Strategy:** Target "How to build [Historical Piece Name]" and "Free woodworking plans for [Antique Style]" keywords.
- **The "Antique Hunter" App:** A mobile app for people visiting antique stores to "Scan and Build" items they can't afford to buy.
